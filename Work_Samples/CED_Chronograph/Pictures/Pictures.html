The CED M2 Chronograph, released in 2005, has been the official ballistic chronograph for the IPSC, and USPSA for the past 18 years, it incorporates both digital and analog technology. I designed this sensor to work using analog technology to reject slower traveling objects such as flies, and also included an analog calibration sequence in order to automatically adjust sensitivity due to changing light levels.
