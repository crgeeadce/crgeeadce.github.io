This system consists of analog techniques such as light sensors, filtering, voltage regulation and self-calibration, as well as providing a digital output event for the CED M2 ballistic chronograph system. Before this design there were no chronograph sensors that were able to maintain consistent sensitivity in all light levels as well as rejecting things that might cause false readings. I also have heard that this design is the best on the market in its class.
